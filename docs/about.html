About MeAuth

MeAuth is a modern, open-source authenticator app for Android, designed for privacy, security, and simplicity. No ads, no tracking—just a beautiful, reliable way to protect your accounts and passwords.

  • Open Source
  • Biometric Security
  • Password Vault
  • No Ads
